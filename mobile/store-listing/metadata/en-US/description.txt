Humanitec is a platform for automating business processes with AI agents.

Build flows for support, sales, and internal work, connect a corporate knowledge base, and manage contacts in CRM. Team chat and calls keep collaboration in one place.

Features:
— AI agents and flows without unnecessary complexity;
— CRM and relationship graph;
— RAG: documents and semantic search;
— team sync in chat.

The web UI updates from the server: after installing the app you get current functionality without reinstalling from the store, until the native shell changes.
